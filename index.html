Designed for a rural setting

The prototype focuses on practical deployment, clear evidence and a simple first-minute workflow.

📶 Low-connectivity fallback

Creates a compact emergency message containing the category, coordinates and request ID for copying into SMS or another channel.

📍 Nearest-first matching

Uses a transparent distance calculation to rank suitable available responders instead of choosing randomly.

🧩 Easy to extend

The same workflow can later connect clinics, roadside teams, farms, volunteers and official services through verified integrations.
